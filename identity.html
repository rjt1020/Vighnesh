Identity Management Systems

Identity Management Systems are used to identify and record movement of a known individual into a factory / office / residential premises.



The commonest of applications of these systems are:

Access Control Systems

Access Control Systems are used in places where entry is restricted to selected people only. These systems are extensively used in banks, data banks, server rooms and corporate offices.
The access control systems can be any of the following:

  • Bar-coded Cards
  • Magnetic Stripe Cards
  • Proximity Cards
  • Smart Cards
  • Biometric Systems

    • These systems are a combination of access control systems with access control mechanisms like:
  • Tripod Turnstiles
  • Revolving Doors
  • Boom Barriers
  • Coupled with powerful software tools these products can also be used to track visitor traffic density, park sales, park attractions

Personnel Identifier System

Personnel Identifier system can be used to verify, catalog and manage the identity of the people, using their unique fingerprints, who are entering your premises. The system can also be programmed to generate an alarm or any other similar applications, which is very useful for high security areas.

Attendance Monitoring Systems

Attendance Monitoring Systems are a very vital tool that can be used in any commercial or industrial area to keep track of employee attendance. This system can also be integrated to your company computer to automatically update all payrolls and attendance registers.
These systems are usually of the following types, namely: Keypad Based, Card Based, Biometric.
Keypad based systems are used in places where the number of users accessing the resource is low and the access security threat is low. These systems are low cost and easy to operate and install. The application areas for these systems are:
Cashier Cabins in banks Godowns and Store Rooms, Records Rooms.
Card Based Systems are used widely in workplaces, institutions, corporate offices, industries for identification and controlling access of individuals. They are mainly of the following types:

  • Barcode Cards
  • Magnetic Cards
  • Proximity Cards
  • Smart Cards

    • These systems are used in places where high volumes of users are present. These systems are economical and flexible in the way that they can be programmed for use in multiple applications. Some of the common application areas are:

  • Attendance Systems
  • Identity Card
  • Access Control to restricted areas like clean rooms, cash rooms, server rooms
  • Billing Applications in Company Clubs, Canteens
  • Roaming Wallet (Employee Data can be carried in smart cards for big corporates)

    • Biometric Systems use a personal trait like fingerprint, facial image or retina image to identify an individual. These systems are the most accurate in identifying an individual as the unique signatures of the finger or the retina cannot be copied. These devices provide a very high accuracy rate of protection to high security areas. They can also be used in high volume user application.
