Audio - Visual Surveillance

Surveillance is an effective tool to ensure that you get to see everything even if you are not present at the location. Today's high tech systems are capable of delivering anything from routine viewing of customers in a bank to high-end systems, which can zoom into and identify the face of a suspect in a subway. Coupled with recording devices and night¬time vision capabilities these systems provide an uninterrupted eagle eye vision of your most sensitive asset.



CCTV Systems

CCTV Systems provide a complete tool to observe, monitor and record activity in any given place. Combined with any recording device, these products can be used to monitor and record activity at a place. These products are very helpful in places where stocks are kept to prevent theft. They are also helpful in places where one wishes to remotely monitor people. They are extensively used in:

  • Godowns, Super Markets, Showrooms
  • Clinics and Hospitals
  • Corporate Offices, Industrial Areas and Residential Apartments.

Video Recording Systems


Video Recording systems provide a wide range of options apart from just recording the views of multiple cameras.
The following are the features of a DVR / NVR:

  • Multiple Channel Input with or without audio recording
  • Real Time recording with Time Stamp (can be used as evidence in a Court Of Law)
  • Capability of use to detect motion in restricted areas
  • Capable to generate output for Siren, EM Locks and other alarm systems
  • Remote monitoring of any place via the Internet or a normal telephone line.
  • Available in stand-alone or PC based forms. The images can also be recorded in a computer for viewing later.
  • Audio and Video output can be used as feed for another monitor / TV or other such viewing device.

Residential Intrusion Shield


The lights and sounds of the city bring along with it the ever-increasing menace of crime. One of the major fallouts of urbanization is the growth in criminal activities within residential areas. To counter these threats our range of products provide various options for a safer lifestyle and secure personal property. Ergonomically designed and thoughtfully manufactured these products provide an indispensable shield against unwanted intruders and at the same time a mechanism to avail emergency professional help from Hospitals, Police or the Fire Brigade.


Burglar and Intrusion Alarm Systems (BIAS)


BIAS is a very powerful tool in corporate and domestic security. This device checks the entry of unauthorized people in a particular area. These systems are available in wired as well as wireless technologies and form a major force in securing banks, offices, godowns and homes.


Laser Security Systems


Laser Security Systems are very easy to use and install products for covering a wide area. This is primarily used to detect motion in a restricted area using laser beams.


Video Door Phones


VDPs provide a very good domestic tool to prevent the entry of unauthorized people into an apartment or homes. These tools can be used prevent forced entry of unknown people in to an apartment or home by selectively allowing entry to people that are known to you.
