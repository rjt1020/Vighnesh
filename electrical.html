Electrical Services

We provide expert services to industrial and domestic consumers, customers and end users who are having HT/LT installations at their premises. We undertake residential, commercial and industrial works. We also undertake Periodic Overhauling (POH) of Power transformers and filtration of transformer oil.
(Services include the Maintenance, Oil Filtration, Installation & Commissioning of Transformers, Relay testing, Breaker installation & Overhauling up to 33 KV. It also includes the Periodic Overhauling (POH) of Power transformers and filtration of transformer oil.)



OUR CORE AREA OF WORK INCLUDES:

    LT works:

  • Design and execution of all types of residential and commercial installation work with testing and commissioning with supply of material
  • Design and execution of coordination for cabling and LT switchgear
  • Preventive and breakdown maintenance of LT switchgear and panel
  • Load sanctioning, liasoning with electricity board and electrical inspector office
  • AMCs for various equipments like pumps etc
  • Design and execution ELV services like data, telephone etc
  • Design and execution for special projects like hospital, schools ,stadium, sports complex etc
  • Lighting system design using simulation software


    • HT works:

  • Overhauling, Maintenance, Erection, Testing & Commissioning of Power Transformer, Distribution Transformer for our various prestigious clients.
  • Erection, Testing & Commissioning of Sub-Station up to 33 KV level & Erection of HT Line up to 33 KV level
  • Overhauling, Maintenance, Testing of Substation & Sub Station Equipments up to 33 KV Class.
  • Handling of commercial Electrification Project along with load sanctioning, liasoning with Electricity Board & Electrical Inspection Office.
  • Supply of Distribution Transformer, small power transformer, Transformer Accessories, HT breaker panel, LT PCC & MCC panels & substation equipments
